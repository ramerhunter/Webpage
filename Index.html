Here is a timeline of ELon Musk's Life:

  • 1971 - Born in South Africa
  • 1991 - Created Blastar Video Game (age of 12)
  • 1995 - Recieved Bachelor in Physics and Economics from the University of Pennsylvania
  • 1999 - Started Zip2 on Feb. 15th, a web software company with only $28,000
  • 1999 - Feb. 28th: sold Zip2 for $22 million
  • 2000 - March: co-founded X.com
  • 2000 - October: Paypal (X.com) was acquired by eBay for $1.5 billion, Elon was given $165 million of that.
  • 2002 - June: Founded Space Exploration Technologies (Space X)
  • 2004 - February: Invested in Tesla Motors and joined Tesla's Board of Directors as its Chairman
  • 2010 - March: Space X launches Falcon 9
  • 2012 - June: Tesla began delivery of its four-door Model S
  • 2013 - August: Musk proposes a new form of transportation between LA and San Francisco via the Hyperloop
  • 2016 - July: Neuralink Corporation was founded
  • 2022 - April: Elon Musk buys Twitter
